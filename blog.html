HOW TO USE VINEGAR FOR HOUSEHOLD CLEANING

In this Quarantine period we have clean our home by our own. Here are some easy tips that you can apply on your cleaning method. Vinegar's acidity is what makes it such a good cleaner. You can use it undiluted, combined with different household objects.
